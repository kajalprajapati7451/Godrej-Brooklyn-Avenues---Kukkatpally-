Godrej Brooklyn Avenue – Brooklyn-Inspired Luxury Living in KPHB, Hyderabad

In a city where connectivity, convenience, and lifestyle have become essential elements of homeownership, Godrej Brooklyn Avenue emerges as a thoughtfully envisioned residential destination in KPHB, Hyderabad. Inspired by the character, creativity, and urban charm associated with Brooklyn, this premium residential community has been designed for modern families who seek more than just an apartment—they seek a complete lifestyle experience.

Located in one of Hyderabad’s most established residential and commercial zones, Godrej Brooklyn Avenue combines contemporary architecture, spacious residences, landscaped open spaces, and premium lifestyle amenities within a well-planned gated community. The development aims to create an environment where residents can enjoy the energy of city life while experiencing the comfort and serenity of a thoughtfully designed neighborhood.

Spread across approximately 7.76 acres, the project introduces a distinctive residential concept that balances modern infrastructure with open green surroundings. From elegant tower elevations to community-centric spaces, every aspect of the development reflects careful planning and attention to detail.

For professionals working in Hyderabad’s thriving IT corridor, families seeking long-term residential stability, and investors looking for a strategically located asset, Godrej Brooklyn Avenue presents an opportunity to be part of a future-ready address in KPHB.

Project Overview

Godrej Brooklyn Avenue has been conceptualized as a large-scale premium residential community designed to meet the evolving expectations of urban homebuyers. The project features two high-rise residential towers rising up to 45 floors, creating a striking architectural presence while offering residents expansive views of the city skyline.

The community includes approximately 1456 residences, offering a range of spacious 3 BHK and 4 BHK homes. Apartment sizes are planned from approximately 1600 sq. ft. to 3200 sq. ft., providing flexibility for different family requirements and lifestyle preferences.

A defining feature of the project is its emphasis on openness. Nearly 75 percent of the development is dedicated to open-to-sky spaces, landscaped gardens, recreational areas, and pedestrian-friendly zones. This planning approach enhances the overall living experience while promoting wellness and community interaction.

At the center of the development is a grand clubhouse spanning approximately 72,000 sq. ft. across multiple levels. Designed as a lifestyle hub, the clubhouse is expected to bring together fitness, recreation, social engagement, and wellness facilities under one roof.
